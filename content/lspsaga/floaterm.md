## Usage

The command is `:Lspsaga term_toggle`.

![Float terminal](https://github.com/nvimdev/lspsaga.nvim/assets/41671631/544b7996-42c7-4c4f-806a-a8c670e9e829)

## Bind a Keymap

```lua
vim.keymap.set({'n','t', '<A-d>', '<cmd>Lspsaga term_toggle'})
```
